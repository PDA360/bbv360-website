Beyond Builders 360° is a globally active venture studio and innovation ecosystem — purpose-built for the Architecture, Engineering, and Construction (AEC) industry.

"We go beyond the ordinary. Beyond Builders 360° is not just a studio — it is a movement to transform the built world through structured innovation."

Our model combines incubation (LAB), acceleration (BOOSTER), and venture capital (HUB) along the full construction lifecycle — from PlanTech in design, ConTech in execution, PropTech in operations, to revitalization technologies.

We are the strategic partner for founders, investors, and AEC organizations that want to shape the future of building — not merely react to it.

The AEC Lifecycle

WE COVER THE
FULL 360°.

Our investment and support activities span all four phases of the construction lifecycle — because innovation is needed at every stage of the built world.

1
0–3 O'CLOCK
PLAN
PlanTech
Concept, feasibility, project development, site acquisition. Technologies transforming how buildings are imagined — BIM, AI design tools, feasibility automation.
2
3–6 O'CLOCK
BUILD
ConTech
Construction execution, quality management, site supervision. Robotics, modular systems, IoT monitoring, digital twins, smart materials, prefabrication.
3
6–9 O'CLOCK
OPERATE
PropTech
Facility and asset management, energy efficiency, tenant experience, leasing, portfolio analytics. Optimizing how buildings perform over their lifetime.
4
9–12 O'CLOCK
REVITALIZE
RevTech
Modernization, adaptive reuse, decarbonization, circular demolition, recycling. Extending the life and value of existing structures sustainably.

Beyond Builders 360° ist ein global aktives Venture Studio und Innovationsökosystem — speziell für die Architektur-, Ingenieur- und Bauindustrie (AEC) entwickelt.

„Wir gehen über das Gewöhnliche hinaus. Beyond Builders 360° ist nicht nur ein Studio — es ist eine Bewegung, um die gebaute Welt durch strukturierte Innovation zu transformieren."

Unser Modell kombiniert Inkubation (LAB), Acceleration (BOOSTER) und Venture Capital (HUB) entlang des gesamten Baulebenszyklus — von PlanTech in der Planung, ConTech in der Ausführung, PropTech im Betrieb bis hin zu Revitalisierungstechnologien.

Wir sind der strategische Partner für Gründer, Investoren und AEC-Organisationen, die die Zukunft des Bauens aktiv gestalten wollen — nicht nur darauf reagieren.
